Islamabad, Pakistan's serene and well-planned capital, offers a fascinating contrast of natural beauty and architectural sophistication. Nestled against the stunning backdrop of the Margalla Hills, this urban landscape is underscored by a network of well-maintained highways. These ribbons of asphalt crisscross the cityscape, adding a touch of modernity to the vibrant verdant surroundings. While cruising down these highways, one can't help but marvel at the juxtaposition of the bustling city life against the tranquil serenity of the mountainous backdrop.
